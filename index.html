Folklore map

Sharing content about heritage

How does it works?

This map aims to share cultural content from around the world. Rather than creating our own content to explain each culture, we are simply sharing local creators.
Explore the map or select a theme from the list.
If you like a content and want to know more about it, just visit the creator profile and check their other videos!
New items will be added over time following this guideline:
  • When possible we will use video from creators who actively share about their culture.
  • Some video will have mutliple theme, a dance video often contains traditional clothes as well, the theme is set as the main content's purpose.
  • The pin on the map does not correspond to actual video's location but where the represented tradition comes from
